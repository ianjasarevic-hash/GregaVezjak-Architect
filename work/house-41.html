HOUSE 41

Location

Velike Zablje, Slovenia

Year

2020

Status

built

House 41 is a project for a family house that builds with an existing old house on the small, narrow plot in the culturally protected old village in the Mediterranean part of Slovenia.

Hiša 41 je projekt družinske hiše, zasnovane ob obstoječi stari hiši na majhni in ozki parceli v kulturno zaščitenem starem vaščanskem jedru v sredozemskem delu Slovenije.

The decision was made with the client to protect old stone house, that was family house for generations. However the client wish is to have larger house with more privacy. All requirement proved very difficult to realise in the small plot.

Skupaj z naročnikom je bila sprejeta odločitev, da se ohrani stara kamnita hiša, ki je bila skozi generacije družinski dom. Hkrati pa je bila želja naročnika večja hiša z več zasebnosti. Vse zahteve je bilo zaradi majhne parcele zelo težko uresničiti.

Solution was proposed to retain old house in a form of its mass, stone load bearing walls and include it in the new house as a courtyard, atrium. New structure is build around the old. What was inside becomes outside and what was outside transforms into inside.

Predlagana rešitev je bila ohranitev stare hiše v obliki njenega volumna in kamnitih nosilnih zidov ter vključitev v novo hišo kot dvorišče oziroma atrij. Nova struktura je zgrajena okoli stare. Kar je bilo nekoč znotraj, postane zunaj, in kar je bilo zunaj, se preoblikuje v notranjost.

Around the atrium the living areas are formed in a way they can communicate with outdoor space as their extension and courtyard becomes double-height multi-functional living room and a garden of the house. In the second floor are sleeping areas and the roof becomes the terrace that compensates small plot with a views to the church, castle and beautiful medieval town on the next hill.

Okoli atrija so oblikovani bivalni prostori, ki lahko komunicirajo z zunanjim prostorom kot njegovim podaljškom, medtem ko dvorišče postane večnamenski dnevni prostor dvojne višine in vrt hiše. V zgornjem nadstropju so spalni prostori, streha pa postane terasa, ki nadomesti majhnost parcele z razgledi na cerkev, grad in čudovito srednjeveško mestece na sosednjem griču.

In this way all wishes were successfully resolved in a new, innovative way: larger house with privacy and at the same time, old house walls were saved. The solution with atrium has also references in the old Mediterranean towns from the antiquity.

Na ta način so bile vse želje uspešno rešene na nov in inovativen način: nastala je večja hiša z več zasebnosti, hkrati pa so bili ohranjeni zidovi stare hiše. Rešitev z atrijem se navezuje tudi na tradicijo starih sredozemskih mest iz antičnega obdobja.
