Design by Grega Vezjak Chosen for Canterbury Earthquake Memorial

Slovenian architect Grega Vezjak's design "The Memorial Wall" was selected from six shortlisted designs submitted from 37 countries.

Oblikovanje slovenskega arhitekta Grega Vezjaka "Spominski zid" je bilo izbrano med šestimi finalnimi projekti, prijavljenimi iz 37 držav.

Slovenian architect Grega Vezjak's design "The Memorial Wall" was selected from six shortlisted designs submitted from 37 countries.

Oblikovanje slovenskega arhitekta Grega Vezjaka "Spominski zid" je bilo izbrano med šestimi finalnimi projekti, prijavljenimi iz 37 držav.

The memorial will be built along the Ōtākaro/Avon River between Montreal Street and Rhododendron Island. It honors the 185 people who died in the February 22, 2011 earthquake.

Spomenik bo zgrajen ob reki Ōtākaro/Avon med Montreal Street in Rhododendron Island. Časti 185 ljudi, ki so umrli v potresu 22. februarja 2011.

Funding includes up to $10 million from the Government and $1 million from the Christchurch Earthquake Mayoral Relief Fund.

Financiranje vključuje do 10 milijonov USD od vlade in 1 milijon USD od Christchurch Earthquake Mayoral Relief Fund.

"I think it will be a stunning tribute to the victims of Canterbury's earthquakes, those who suffered through the quakes, and the courage of those who participated in the rescue and recovery operations."

"Menim, da bo to čudovit spomenik žrtvam canterburyjskih potresov, tistim, ki so prestali potrese, in pogum tistih, ki so sodelovali pri reševalnih in obnovitvenih operacijah."
