Biography

Biografija

Grega Vezjak was born in 1981 and grew up in Bilje, Slovenia. After graduation in 2007 at Faculty of Architecture, University of Ljubljana, he worked in architectural firms, collaborating on a variety of projects from large commercial high rise building to smaller residential and community projects in both public and private sector. In 2013 he became a member of the Slovenian Chamber of Architecture and Spatial Planning (ZAPS) and began working as a sole practitioner.

Grega Vezjak se je rodil leta 1981 in odraščal v Biljah, Slovenija. Po diplomi leta 2007 na Fakulteti za arhitekturo, Univerza v Ljubljani, je delal v arhitekturnih firmah, sodeloval je na različnih projektih od velikih komercialnih stolpnic do manjših stanovanjskih in skupnostnih projektov v javnem in zasebnem sektorju. Leta 2013 je postal član Slovenske zbornice za arhitekturo in prostorsko načrtovanje (ZAPS) in začel delati kot samostojen praktik.

Awards and achievements include producing the winning design for a coat of arms of Orehovlje competition and coming second in a partnership design in a national architectural competition for designing Student housing in Nova Gorica.

Nagrade in dosežki vključujejo zmago v oblikovanju grba za tekmovanje Orehovlje in drugo mesto v partnerskem oblikovanju na nacionalnem arhitekturnem tekmovanju za načrtovanje študentskega naselja v Novi Gorici.

In May 2015, Grega’s design won the international competition for the Canterbury Earthquake Memorial to be built in Christchurch, New Zealand. His design was selected from more than 330 international entries. In less than two years of ongoing work, in February 2017 the Canterbury Earthquake National Memorial – Oi Manawa was officially opened.

maja 2015 je Gregov projekt zmagal na mednarodnem tekmovanju za spomenik za potres na Canterburyju, ki naj bi bil zgrajen v Christchurchu, Nova Zelandija. Njegov projekt je bil izbran med več kot 330 mednarodnimi prijavami. V manj kot dveh letih dela je bil februarja 2017 uradno odprt Nacionalni spomenik za potres na Canterburyju – Oi Manawa.

In January 2017 his design was selected from 128 entries in an international competition to build Tri-An Monument in Louisville, KY, United States. The Monument was completed and dedicated in October 2023.

Januarja 2017 je bil njegov projekt izmed 128 prijav izbran na mednarodnem tekmovanju za gradnjo spomenika Tri-An v Louisvilleju, Kentucky, Združene države. Spomenik je bil dokončan in posvečen oktobra 2023.

In April 2018 his design for the Pan-European Memorial for the Victims of Totalitarianism in Brussels was shortlisted in the top 10 entries in the international competition.

aprila 2018 je bil njegov projekt za Pan-evropski spomenik za žrtve totalitarizma v Bruslju uvrščen med top 10 prijav na mednarodnem tekmovanju.
